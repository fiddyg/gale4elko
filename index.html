Welcome!

Hello and welcome to the Gale4Elko blog. My name is Brian Gale, and my intention is to explore and share concepts related to education. I sincerely believe that there are viable solutions to every challenge we have faced, and every challenge we will face in the preparation of our youth for successful participation in society.

I'm currently running for a seat on the Elko County School Board of Directors. I would appreciate your support in that race, but even more I would appreciate your participation, by sharing your thoughts with me. Together, I know we can prepare our youth for success in their own lives.
